Highlights about the Course

Who this course is for?

  • This course is ideal for experienced QA professionals, performance testers, and developers looking to specialize in performance testing using LoadRunner. It is also beneficial for those aiming to enhance their skills in load, stress, and scalability testing.

Why take this course?

  • Learning this course helps you master advanced performance testing techniques with LoadRunner, ensuring applications perform optimally under various loads. It equips you with the skills to identify and resolve performance issues, improving software quality and user experience.

What you will learn?

  • The course covers advanced LoadRunner techniques, including scripting, load and stress testing, and protocol handling. You'll also learn how to analyze performance results and optimize applications for scalability and efficiency.
